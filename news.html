News About Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana

Women Applicants Increase under PMAY

In a good news to women home buyers across the country, it has been found that a number of banks are offering special interest rates in an effort to encourage more and more women to purchase homes. It is mandatory under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY) for the property to be registered in the name of a female owner. There has been a 6% rise in the number of women home loan applicants under PMAY. Not just this, to offer more support to women, an extra stamp duty concession is also being given to women if the property is registered under a female name. Many well-known banks are offering special interest rates exclusively for women. On the whole, a woman who is applying for a loan can get a discount in interest rate of up to 0.05% per annum when compared to men. Banks such as SBI, HDFC and SBI offer special home loan interest rates for women. As far as PMAY is concerned, the total subsidy that can be claimed adds up to Rs.2.67 lakh. Under the PMAY scheme, the aim is to encourage women to own property and this, in turn, will help them become more empowered. Subsidies will be provided under this scheme for all home loan applications made by women borrowers.

Manali New Town to See Nearly 3,000 Houses Under PMAY

The Government of Jammu and Kashmir is going to prepare a new list of the homeless individuals in the state. This announcement was made my Abdul Haq Khan, the Rural Development Minister. This particular list of homeless individuals is being prepared under the PMAY or Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana aiming to provide housing for all by 2022. Abdul Haq Khan made the announcement after inaugurating the first construction project under PMAY in Samba. The Housing for All by 2022 scheme under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana was introduced in the year 2015 for making homes affordable for individuals belonging to Economically Weaker Section (EWS), Low Income Group (LIG), and Middle Income Group (MIG 1 & 2).
